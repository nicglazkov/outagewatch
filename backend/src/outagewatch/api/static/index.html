Questions

Is this an official PG&E service?

No. OutageWatch is not affiliated with PG&E. It reads PG&E's public outage map and can lag behind reality. For emergencies call 911. Report downed power lines to PG&E at 1-800-743-5000.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free, with no account and no ads.

How accurate are the restoration times?

They come straight from PG&E's feed and can change or slip. We show them as given and flag when an estimate has already passed.

Which areas are covered?

PG&E territory in Northern and Central California. If you enter a ZIP served by a different utility, we will tell you.

Do you track me?

No analytics, no tracking cookies, no ads. See the Privacy Policy.
