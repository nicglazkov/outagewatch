How it works

Only what matters

Alerts fire when an outage starts, when the restoration estimate moves, when power returns, and for planned safety shutoffs. An address only alerts if the outage actually reaches it.

Plain-language answers

Each outage gets a short, calm explanation of what is going on and when power is expected back, drawn from the real feed.

No account, no ads

Free to use. No sign-up, no tracking. Your saved area stays on your device.

Questions

Is this an official PG&E service?

No. OutageWatch is not affiliated with PG&E. It reads PG&E's public outage map and can lag behind reality. For emergencies call 911. Report downed power lines to PG&E at 1-800-743-5000.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free, with no account and no ads.

How accurate are the restoration times?

They come from PG&E's feed and can change or slip. We show them as given and flag when an estimate has already passed.

Do you track me?

No analytics, no tracking cookies, no ads. See the Privacy Policy.
